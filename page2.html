Hi, I'm Joy Quiyote

I was born in Madison, Wisconsin and raised with a creative mother who never thought art would become a job.  And it hasn't!  As the old adage goes, if you are doing something you love, you are not working. I love to travel and enjoy all the flavors of the world has to offer.

I think I have been creating designs my entire life, whether it be in dream, sketching, painting, food, grooming dogs (which I call 3-D modeling pet enhancement) or digital. I am inspired by music, films, street art and of course, other artists. Within each of us, there is an artist waiting to be set free and I routinely try to break the constraints of what I have been told is possible.

The purpose of my designs is to impact each viewer in a different way. I will always try to leave a little to the imagination and help the viewer see through the lens I try to create. Each design has a different feel and each stroke or pixel has a different purpose, but together they make a story worth telling every time.
